About

Apollo is an autonomousdriving opensource platform by Baidu. The software aim to provide developers with tools to help them bring autonomous driving products to market.

The official site is here.

Apollo Open Platform

The open-source architecutre is available on Github.

Apollo Components

Accurate Perception -- An online perception module and cloud-based calibration service.
Simulation -- A comprehensive solution for the development of autonomous vehicles.
HD Map and Localization -- The HD mapping data provide by Chinese firm Baidu.
Planning -- Apollo vehicles are equipped with a planning system consisting of prediction, behavior, and motion logic.
Intelligent Control -- The precise Apollo intelligent vehicle control and canbus-proxy modules.
Apollo Dataset -- The Apollo Autonomous Vehicle Dataset
